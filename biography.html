Biography

I'm an Interactive Arts and Technology design student pursuing my degree in Simon Fraser University. I was born in Japan, and raised in China and Canada. I grew interested in graphics design when I was in my teens when looking through social media content and wondering how everything was made. After highschool, I intially started my education in Computer Science, but later decided to make the change to Interactive Arts and Technology after discovering it in University. Currently I'm studying Web Design, Game Design, and 3D moddeling this semster, and I'm hoping to engage in new experience to develop my personal abilities.


In my personal life, I like to build online communities in games that I play, and have good organization skills and am easy to work with. I've also been heavily invested in the eSports professional side of League of Legends, through playing and coaching for teams in the SFU eSports association. I'm currently employed part time at a Korean supermarket called "assimart".

Skills

Some of the skills that I have are Coding in Python, C++, and Java. And being able to use most of the Adobe products, like Photoshop, Premier, and Illustrator. I'm also media literate, understanding how most of the social medias works.
